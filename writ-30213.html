Personal Statement

In this course, I studied rhetoric within the context of social media. I completed one major project for each of the sections of the course. Throughout the semester, I also contributed to a class Facebook page, by posting relevant and interesting pieces of, or articles relating to, social media. To view a sample of these posts, click (here).

 

Unit One focused on identity and identification, and I wrote an authoethnographical research paper on social media and news-sourcing. (I featured this piece on my “Other Work” page, but it also appears at right.)

 

Unit Two focused on exploring rhetorical situations, and I performed a case study of social media rhetoric, reporting my research essay-style.

 

Unit Three, my favorite unit, focused on rhetorical action, and I got the opportunity to create a simulative social media campaign.
