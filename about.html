Personal Statement

My name is Mckenzie Marciante. I am a junior writing major with minors in both anthropology and Italian at Texas Christian University, and I intend to graduate in May of 2018.

 

In addition to attending school full-time, I work part-time both at TCU's writing center as a peer writing consultant, and at Byrd & Bleecker, a custom cards and stationary shop, as an associate. Immediately after completing my undergraduate degree, I plan to teach middle school English and coach track and field while attending graduate school for graphic design.

 

My long-term career goals include long-form journalism and photojournalism and, ultimately, magazine editing.
